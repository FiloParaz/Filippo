Iphone 2G

When

The first iphone was born on January 9, 2007, but officially entered the market in 2010.

Porpuse of use

The iphone 2G it was a revolution in the world of technology because it didn’t make calls and send messages, but it could offer videos, photos, maps, music and many other services for example:

  • transport;
  • mobile bank;
  • Online shopping;
  • book travel and holidays;
  • order food.

Dimension

The iphone 2G it had a unique appearance, it was equipped with a 3.5-inch LCD display with a resolution of 320x480 pixels.
It was then equipped with an ARM processor produced by Samsung, with 128MB of RAM memory.

Popularity

When Apple released its first iPhone it wasn't only the first smartphone in the history.
Many smartphone companies were competing with each other to capture the market share.
Iphone becomes popular when we knew about its quality features, his material and his potential.
Apple sold the one millionth number of Iphone 74 days after its release in USA.
The iPhone was a hit because it was the first of an entirely new kind of personal computer.
An always-on always-connected pocketable device, capable of running all manner of applications.

Price

At the release day, in USA in 2007 you could find the iphone at the price of $499 for the 4GB model and $599 for the 8 GB model, it was very expensive.
The price included 2 years of warranty.

Style

Iphone was available in only 2 colours: black and white.

The kind of technology

the iPhone 2G used many terms, including: touch screen, 2G, bluetooth, wi-fi (internet), streaming and social media.
it is a combination of Ipod (music player) with touch, revolutionary mobile phone and internet.

Age of users

the device was designed for teenagers, in fact it was created to give the daughter of steve job "unfinished" music and not the usual cassettes (small curiosity that emerges from the film).
But the age of users ranged from 33 to 40 years.

Time spent

thanks to technologies such as streaming and social media, the time spent on the smartphone reached 203 minutes in 24 hours.
